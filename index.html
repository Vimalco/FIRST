Campus Life

World-Class Facilities

We invest in environments where every student can thrive — intellectually, physically, and socially.

Research Library

Over 4 million physical volumes and 12 million digital resources. Open 24/7 with silent study zones, collaboration rooms, and specialist librarians.

Advanced Laboratories

State-of-the-art wet labs, robotics suites, quantum computing clusters, and maker spaces equipped with the latest scientific instruments.

Sports & Wellness

Olympic swimming pool, indoor arena, climbing wall, tennis courts, and a dedicated wellness center offering yoga, therapy, and nutrition coaching.

Student Residences

Modern on-campus housing for 18,000 students with co-working spaces, rooftop gardens, and fast fiber internet in every room.

Dining & Culture

12 dining venues serving cuisines from around the world. Dedicated halal, vegan, and allergen-free kitchens, and weekly international food festivals.

Digital Campus

Campus-wide gigabit Wi-Fi, cloud storage for every student, AI tutoring tools, and a dedicated tech support centre open every day of the year.
